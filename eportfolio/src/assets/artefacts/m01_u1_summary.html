This unit introduces students to the roles, responsibilities of IT professionals, examines various threats encountered and look at the role of a code of conduct in IT practices.
  1. Roles include programmers, data analysts, leadership and scientisys. Skills required include leadership, technical and analytical thinking.
  2. Industry threats include distributed data, data hacking, data leaks (related to intellectual property), cyber terrorism, data privacy, breaches in data confidentiality, an increase in always on mentality and adoption of artificial intelligence (AI).
  3. Data threats have ethical and legal implications. IT professionals should adhere to BCS Code of Conduct and be mindful of GDPR Data Protection Regulation.

Students were encouraged to discuss ethics of data protection via GDPR and their organisation's own Code of Conduct.
